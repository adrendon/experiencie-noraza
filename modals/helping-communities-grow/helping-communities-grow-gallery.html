The Adame Yirgachefe School, Ethiopia

The Costa Foundation first became aware of Adame Yirgachefe 2nd Cycle School in January 2011. The school was in a dreadful state with up to 90 children crammed in a classroom and the structure being very poor.

After a lot of hard work, the new school opened in October 2011 and has been wonderfully received by the children, parents and teachers. Read the case study at the Costa Foundation website.
