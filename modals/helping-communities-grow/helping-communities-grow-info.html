Life Changing

The Costa Foundation is a “Golden Pound” charity where all donations received are used to benefit these important life-changing projects. Company funds cover the costs of running the charity.

To date:

  • 26,000 lives all transformed thanks to you.
  • 264 classrooms all built in 41 coffee growing communities.
  • 41 school projects built in 8 countries across 3 continents

...all this in just 7 years.

You Can Make a Difference too. The money you give goes towards funding projects in the coffee-growing communities we are supporting. You can donate online or via SMS, please visit the following link for more details: www.costafoundation.com/donations/
