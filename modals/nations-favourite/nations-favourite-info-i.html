A big thank you to all our coffee lovers. Costa was voted the cream of the coffee shops for the third year in a row!

The most recent Allegra Strategies survey, (believed to be the largest ever independent survey of the coffee market) identified Costa as the UK's favourite coffee shop. And this isn't the first time; Costa also took the top spot for the last two years.

Allegra Strategies is a London-based strategic research consultancy recognised as a global leader in the foodservice and coffee shop sector. Allegra is best known for its definitive reports on the European and UK coffee markets. They have accurately predicted trends in the UK branded coffee shop market since 1999.
